Through crowdfunding, we hope to increase the income and output of our fishermen.

Through the process of crowdfunding, potential investors in Philnance are partnering directly to impact the lives of the fishermen by increasing their capability to farm or catch fish.

  • Philnance is a fundraising company that specializes in the process of crowdfunding.
  • Our selected sector in helping the community is the sector of fishing.
  • A net profit pattern is observed in our investment plans.

You can be a pioneer of hope for the fishermen.

Learn on how you can be part of changing the lives of our fellow fisherman through our investment programs.

Supported by Different Nations

Philnance was started locally in the Philippines, but nations like the United States and Singapore have already supported the organization.

Being socially responsible

Philnance gives a direct opportunity to potential investors on building the social community of fishermen.

Investing and Rewarding

In Philnance, investing is rewarding, not just socially but also in finance. Sustainability and scalability are the top keypoint in our investment plan.

Environmental Friendly

Through the support that Philnance gives to our fishermen, they now have the capability to catch more fish in a legal and environmentally friendly way.
